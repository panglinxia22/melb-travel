Meet our Team

Our team is full of people that are extremely passionate about making your Australian journey as good as it possibly can be. They have travel experience as travellers, but also within the industry too. Our team has over 35+ years of travel industry experience, meaning we really are Australia experts and you’re in the best possible hands.

Guide Team

Our guides make sure you have the most incredible time on tour. Throughout the week, they’ll be showing you around the city, sharing important cultural knowledge, giving their best travel tips and will essentially become your new favourite older brother or sister!

Community Team

Need help with travel plans, someone to settle your nerves or general questions about Australia? The community team will be your go to. Our incredible team all have experience travelling Australia and around the world, making them the perfect people to help plan your onward adventures after your Welcome to Travel tour!

Reservations Team

The reservations team are the reason you can enjoy your tour stress free. From booking your hostels, activities, amending date changes and booking any extra nights, reservations will make sure you know exactly where you need to be from the moment you step off the plane to the very end of your tour.

Marketing & Bookkeeping Team

Did you first hear about Welcome to Travel on social? The marketing team do their best to help inspire future backpackers to make the ultimate decision and book the trip of a lifetime to Australia! Our bookkeeping team makes sure everything runs smoothly behind the scenes.
